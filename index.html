horyzon (h)

the future of collaboration

blockchain – the underlying technology of bitcoin, allows for the transfer of value to anyone in the world.

this revolutionary technology is redifining how we percieve value and ownership. value systems once afforded only to governments can now be created by anyone, completely decentralised and free of single control.

one such value system is equity. in the traditional sense, equity is ownership in a company, and to be recognised legally it must be maintained and regulated by government intermediaries within territories and states. at an abstract level, it is a piece of the future in which humans collaborate around and invest significant time and effort to manifest it into something real.

by using blockchain, this abstract definition can be encapsulated more purely. by generating a currency of cryptoequity for a project, you can pay people in these pieces of the future, allowing collaboration at a scale never seen before. say goodbye to the monetary and network constraints of the past. whether it be a song or an app, you can search the horyzon for collaborators and share in the creation of tomorrow.

it is very early days, there will inevitably be a wrong turn taken here and there, but fuck it, that's where the fun is. if you're reading this – let's create together, join the horyzon community here slack, get a beta invite and spread the word :)

the social (ts)

young people. cool shit.

an online publication local to the inner suburbs of sydney. created in an effort to unite what feels like an ironically disconnected generation. with a group of friends, we went out to find the countless people our age building companies, publishing blogs, taking photos, painting canvases and actively creating every day. this resonated, garnering a following of 2000+ locals.

the social has been a lesson in the obvious mantra of 'substance over style'. we leaned on our digital aesthetic whilst we rapidly tried to grasp with the arduous task of maintaining regular content with limited money coming in. it still stands with a few pieces of gold lying around, but sits to the side as i focus on other projects. i hope to revive it.
